1. Shipping & Delivery
How long will it take to get my order?
Standard shipping typically takes 5–7 business days. If you’ve opted for express shipping, you can expect your items within 2–3 business days. You’ll receive a tracking link via email as soon as your order hits the road.

Do you ship internationally?
Yes! We love sharing our boho vibes worldwide. International shipping times vary by location, usually arriving within 10–15 business days.

Can I change my shipping address after placing an order?
We process orders quickly, but if you catch us within 2 hours of placing your order, we can usually update the address. Shoot us an email with your order number immediately.

2. Returns & Exchanges
What is your return policy?
We want you to love what you bought. If you’re not feeling it, you can return unworn and unwashed items within 30 days of delivery.

How do I start a return?
Just visit our Returns Portal [Link], enter your order number and email, and follow the instructions to print your prepaid shipping label.

When will I receive my refund?
Once we receive your return, please allow 5–7 business days for the refund to reflect in your original payment method.

3. Orders & Payments
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, AMEX), PayPal, Apple Pay, and Google Pay.

Can I cancel my order?
Orders can be cancelled within 1 hour of purchase. After that, the fulfillment process has likely begun, and you’ll need to follow the return process once the item arrives.

My order arrived damaged—what should I do?
We are so sorry! Please take a photo of the damaged item and the packaging, then email it to our support team at jnvhimanshu11@gmail.com so we can send a replacement right away.

4. Product & Sizing
How do I know which size to pick?
Every product page features a detailed Size Guide. Our items generally run true to size, but if you’re between sizes, we usually recommend sizing up for that relaxed boho fit.

How should I care for my items?
To keep your pieces looking fresh, we recommend washing them in cold water on a gentle cycle and hanging them to dry. Avoid bleach and high-heat dryers.

5. Sustainability & Ethics
Are your products ethically sourced?
Absolutely. We partner with manufacturers who prioritize fair wages and safe working conditions. We believe in "slow fashion" that lasts.

Is your packaging eco-friendly?
Yes! We use recycled and biodegradable mailers whenever possible to minimize our footprint on the planet.
